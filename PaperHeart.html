How to make a paper heart

Instructions

  1. Fold your paper in half to form a triangle.
  2. Open it and fold it half again the other way.
  3. Fold the top point down to meet the center where your previous folds meet.
  4. Take the bottom point and fold it up to meet the top of your paper.
  5. Take the right-hand side of your paper and fold it up to meet the center crease. The edge should be touching the center crease.
  6. Do the same on the left.
  7. Flip your heart over and fold over the left and right points.
  8. Do the same for the top two points.
