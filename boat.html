How to make an origami boat.

Instructions

  1. Position the paper vertically(long edges on the left and right).
  2. Fold the bottom half up so that the short edges meet.
  3. Fold the paper in half horizontally(i.e. from left to right or right to left).
  4. Unfold the fold made in the previous step.
  5. Fold the right corner towards the middle so that it meets the middle line.
  6. Repeat the previous step with the left corner.
  7. Rotate the paper so that it's upside down.
  8. Fold one layer frmo the bottom up.
  9. Flip the paper over to the other side.
  10. Fold the bottom edge up in the same way.
  11. Unfold, and then fold the bottom right corner along the crease made.
  12. Repeat on the bottom left flap.
  13. Re-fold the bottom edge back up.
  14. Open the bottom of the model.
  15. Insert the overlapping flap on the left underneath the right section.
  16. Fold one layer up.
  17. Flip over the paper and repeat the previous step.
  18. Open the bottom of the model.
  19. Pull apart the left and right flaps.
