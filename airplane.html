How to make a paper airplane

Instructions

  1. Fold the paper in half vertically.
  2. Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.
  3. Repeat step 2 with the top of the new edges.
  4. Fold the paper in half the same way as step 1
  5. Fold down the wings so that the top of the wing and the bottom of the base meet
